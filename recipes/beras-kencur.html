Description

Beras kencur is a traditional Indonesian drink made from rice, kencur (aromatic ginger), palm sugar, and tamarind. It has a sweet, slightly spicy, and refreshing taste, often enjoyed cold. The rice gives it a smooth, milky texture, while kencur provides a distinctive earthy aroma and warming sensation.

This drink is widely enjoyed across Indonesia as both a refreshment and a wellness beverage. Its natural ingredients are believed to help increase energy, support digestion, and relieve tiredness, making beras kencur a popular choice in everyday life.

Ingredients

  • 1500 ml clean water
  • 150 grams palm sugar
  • 125 grams aromatic ginger
  • 50 grams rice
  • 5 tsp sugar
  • 5 pieces of ginger
  • Tamarind as needed

Steps

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly and soak it in clean water for about 1 hour.
  2. Boil palm sugar, granulated sugar, tamarind, and ginger in water until dissolved, then let the mixture cool.
  3. Clean, peel, and chop the kencur.
  4. Drain the soaked rice.
  5. Blend the rice, kencur, and cooled tamarind mixture until smooth.
  6. Strain the mixture to remove any pulp.
  7. Serve the beras kencur drink
