Perkedel

Home

Description

Perkedel is a traditional Indonesian fritter made from the union of mashed potatoes and eggs (persatuan kentang dan telur). Boiled or fried potatoes are mashed until smooth, then mixed with beaten egg to bind the mixture into a soft, cohesive dough.

Ingredients

  • 400 grams of potatoes (about 3 large ones)
  • 5 shallots
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 spring onion, finely sliced
  • 1 stalk of celery, finely chopped
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • 1 tsp ground pepper
  • 1 egg white, lightly beaten

Steps

  1. Peel, wash, and chop the potatoes.
  2. Fry the potatoes with the shallots and garlic, then drain.
  3. Puree the shallots and garlic and set aside.
  4. Mash the fries.
  5. Mix the mashed potatoes with the egg yolks, ground spices, sugar, salt, spring onions, and pepper.
  6. Stir until well combined.
  7. Form into flat balls (approximately 2 tablespoons).
  8. Dip in the egg white.
  9. Fry until golden brown.
  10. Serve.
